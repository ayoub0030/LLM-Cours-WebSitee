What does LLM stand for?

Large Language Model - an AI trained on vast amounts of text to understand and generate human language.

What is 'Tokenization' in LLMs?

Breaking down input text into smaller pieces (tokens) like words or sub-words that the model can process.

What key technology introduced in 2017 revolutionized LLMs?

The Transformer architecture, introduced in the "Attention is All You Need" paper by Google researchers.

What is the 'Attention Mechanism' primarily used for?

To help the model understand context by weighing the importance of different words in relation to each other.

What does it mean if an LLM 'hallucinates'?

It generates incorrect or nonsensical information that wasn't in its training data, essentially making things up.

What is 'Fine-tuning' an LLM?

Training a pre-trained LLM further on a smaller, specific dataset to specialize its knowledge or task performance.
